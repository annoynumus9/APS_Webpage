Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Auro! Incorporated, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our verification service.

2. Data Collection

When using our service, we collect the following:

  • Discord ID: Used for identification purposes.
  • Roblox ID: Linked with your Discord ID for verification.

3. Data Use

We use your data to:

  • Verify your Roblox account with your Discord account.
  • Ensure the integrity of the verification process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your Discord and Roblox IDs as long as you are using our verification system. However, if you request to delete your data, we will remove all personal data except for your Discord and Roblox IDs, which will be retained for verification purposes.

5. Data Deletion

If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us directly through auroraplasmasystems@gmail.com. You may request the deletion of all your data, but we will still retain your Discord ID and Roblox ID for security and verification purposes.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security due to the nature of the internet.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about your data or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at auroraplasmasystems@gmail.com.
